Motive

“No one truly understands the importance of blood donations – until someone you know receives it”..
When we asked why they have not donated,many people say it is because they were never asked to donate.
Let’s make that reason an excuse of the past, shall we ? Blood donation is a community responsibility.
While you're reading this,A local area patient needs blood.Our blood supply comes from caring donors
like you.It takes about one hour of your time.When you give blood,It gives someone another smile,another
hug,another chance. It is the gift of life. A blood donation is one of the greatest gifts that a person
can give.It is a selfless act that can have a long-lasting impact on another person’s life.A decision to
donate your blood can save a life, or even several.


What is this for ?

This site solves the problem of blood emergencies by connecting blood donors directly with people .
in blood need.it is an innovative approach that provides immediate access to blood donors.Our site
ultimate goal is to provide an easy-to-use, easy-to-access, fast, efficient, and reliable way to get
life-saving blood. it works to connect blood donors and recipients through an website.


How does it work ?

Simply register within our website,As soon as registration is done,it is routed among our website
it will alert the donors who are neraby.We know time matters! Instead of having to search all over
for a blood donor in an emergency situation,you can spend your time consoling the patient. We keep
you updated at each step of the request process, from when a volunteer has been found to when blood
is on its way.
